IEEE Innovation Nation 2018 Grand Finale

The final moment of the largest competition in 2018

What will happen..?



Every great innovation starts off with a simple idea and each such idea needs guidance to improvise, finance to develop and an insight on the industry it addresses. This sums up the aims of IEEE Innovation Nation Sri Lanka (INSL) 2018 in providing the young entrepreneurs with essential training, mentorship and seed-funding in order to make way to successful entrepreneurs, hence, establishing the foundation for our nation’s prosperity.

With this in mind, INSL conducted several workshops over the last few months, to selected eight teams, in different aspects of entrepreneurship, so that they can shape up their existing entrepreneurial ideas. We have finally reached the conclusion of the workshop series for our teams. With that, the IEEE Innovation Nation 2018 is ready to move on to the final stage of the competition, the Grand Finale of IEEE Innovation Nation Sri Lanka 2018 unveiled together with IEEE Sri Lanka Section Student/Young Professionals/Women in Engineering Congress 2018.

The winners will be recognized at the IEEE Sri Lanka Section Awards Night 2018 which is happening from 6:30 pm onwards on 29th December 2018
