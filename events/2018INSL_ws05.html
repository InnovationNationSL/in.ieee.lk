Speaker Delegates

Personal Profiles of the Event Speakers

Rohan Jayaweera

Co-founder, Director & COO of Antyra Solutions



Rohan's Personal Profile

Rohan Jayaweera is the director, co-founder as well as the Chief Operations Officer at Antyra Solutions (Pvt.) Ltd., a boutique and exclusive omni­channel digital experience management company.

He was the Country consultant for Google Asia Pacific and currently for Palo Alto Networks. He was a lead management consultant for IT sector based in Singapore and held the leadership positions in Cisco, Lenovo and IBM, across geographies. In addition to that, he was the Country Coordinator ­ for Google outreach initiatives, GBG, GDG, GSA, GBG Women and more.

He is a keynote speaker, presenter and moderator for countless events and was invited to speak at the inaugural TEDx in SL on entrepreneurship as well.

Hasanga Abeyratne

Founder and CEO of Amplifyn


Hasanga's Personal Profile

He is the Founder and CEO at Amplifyn, a UX design consultancy company based in Colombo, specialised in providing user experience and digital product design and engineering services to technology startups and corporates.

He is an experienced digital product designer with a special interest in working with growth focused technology startups and corporates. Further, he is also an advisor on UX and Design for StartupXFoundry.

Hasanga’s wide experience includes having been the Head of Design and AirArabia, UX Architect at the 99X Technology, a Software Engineer at Puzzelpart, Norway and a Software Engineer at Innovative-e Inc among others.
