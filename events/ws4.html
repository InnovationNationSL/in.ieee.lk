Speaker Delegates

Personal Profiles of the Event Speakers

Sushena Ranathunga

Co-founder and Director of Creative Software



Sushena's Personal Profile

Sushena is a co-founder and director of Creative Software since 1999. He is also the founder of Four Corners Lanka and a director / board member of Cambio Software Engineering Pte Ltd.

Sushena has been working in the IT Industry in Sri Lanka and overseas since 1989 as a programmer, analyst programmer, systems analyst, project coordinator, consultant and technical director.

He holds an MSc in Information Technology from Keele University.

Shiraz Latiff

Founder of Hummingbird International


Shiraz's Personal Profile

Shiraz has been a known name in the Strategy, Business design and transformation for the past 28 years.

He founded Hummingbird International, as an Organizational Design & Development consultancy with strategic partnerships globally. He is the Chairman of Consulus Global, an Innovation and Business Design consultancy based out of Singapore with offices in all continents.

He is a Past Chairman of the Chartered Institute of Marketing Sri Lanka Region and a Trustee of CIM UK representing the International Regions. He is also a Director of World Marketing Summit Group Inc among others.
