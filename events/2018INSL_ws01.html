Personal Profiles of the Event Speakers

Yasith Abeynayake

Head of Business Analysis for Cambio Software Engineering



Yasith's Personal Profile

Yasith is a Head of Business Analysis for Cambio Software Engineering, a research & development center for Cambio Healthcare Systems.

He leads a team which connects Design Thinking, Product Design & Business Analysis to design human-centric digital healthcare products. Further he is a co-founder of Design Think Forum.

Nisal Weerakoon

Associate Business Consultant for Sysco Labs


Nisal's Personal Profile

Nisal Weerakoon is an Associate Business Consultant working at Sysco LABS in CAKE Point of Sale Product Team. The CAKE Point of Sale software is a platform-independent cloud-based solution specifically designed and built for the restaurant industry.

He is currently holding Division Director position in Toastmasters International, District 82 (Tamil Nadu & Sri Lanka) and has received Distinguished Toastmaster (DTM) award from Toastmasters International.

He has a BSc. Degree in Computing from Staffordshire University (UK), Masters in Information Technology Degree from University of Colombo School of Computing and is a Certified Scrum Product Owner.
