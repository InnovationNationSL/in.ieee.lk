The Legal Know-How of Startups

The 8th installment of IEEE INSL 2018 workshop series

What will happen..?



Running a business can be tricky, and with the legal issues thrown into the mix, it can become your worst nightmare! To build a corporate world around you, you need to be aware of the nuances of the corporate law!

Hold up! IEEE INSL 2018 is conducting the final workshop installment of the training series on ‘The Legal Know-How of Startups’ on the 13th of December 2018 at Dialog Axiata! Hurry up and Reserve your seat today!

Speaker Delegates

Personal Profiles of the Event Speakers

Lilan Wikramaratne

Multidisciplinary Professional, Attorney- at - Law



Lilan's Personal Profile

Lilan Wickramarathne is a multidisciplinary professional, qualified in law and accountancy, an Attorney- at - Law of the Supreme Court of Sri Lanka and a Registered Company Secretary. He is an Associate Member of the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (ACMA) (UK) and a Chartered Global Management Accountant (CGMA).

Prior to joining Dialog Axiata PLC, he had served as a Tax consultant at KPMG Chartered Accountants and as a Litigator practising in the Corporate law field at F.J. and G de Saram Attorneys at Law. Currently, he is the Unit Manager – Group Legal and Regulatory of Dialog Axiata PLC.

Yushan Gunathilake

Electronic and Telecommunication Engineer



Yushan's Personal Profile

Yushan is an Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering graduate from University of Moratuwa and holds a Masters in Business Administration from the University of Sri Jayewardenepura. He currently heads the sub-units Pricing, Mergers and Acquisitions at Dialog Axiata PLC. While being an Associate Member of CIMA(UK), he also works as a lecturer in the field of Management Accounting for CIMA, ICASL, AAT and CMA.

Mohamed Imran

MSenior Manager in taxation for Dialog Axiata PLC



Imran's Personal Profile

Imran is a Member of ACCA (UK). He studied and received training in the United Kingdom. He has worked as a manager in Tax Compliance and Advisory for EY Colombo tax division. Imran also has worked as Senior Tax Consultant for Deloitte in Saudi Arabia. He currently works as a Senior Manager in taxation for Dialog Axiata PLC.
