Speaker Delegates

Personal Profiles of the Event Speakers

Chalinda Abeykoon

Chief Executive Officer of Crowdisland Pvt Ltd



Chalinda's Personal Profile

He is the Chief Executive Officer of Crowdisland Pvt Ltd, which aims to bring together angel investors and promising startups, to create a structured and efficient mechanism through which startups can get funded.

He is an experienced startup evangelist and strategist constructing the tech startup ecosystem of Sri Lanka, who has acted as one of the key executioners in launching Sri Lanka’s first government-funded incubator and the country’s first industry-focused accelerator in 2010 and 2014 respectively.

During his time at the Information and Communication Technology Agency (ICTA) of Sri Lanka, he was a major foundation for the growth of 22 startups. Further, he has also contributed to promoting Sri Lanka as a niche sourcing destination in Africa, Australia, India and the United Kingdom.

Aloka Gunasekara

Product Development Professional


Aloka's Personal Profile

He is a skilled product development professional with over nine years of experience in strategic leadership, business mentoring, digital strategy, business development and recruitment. He is passionate about startups and the impact these can make by addressing diverse issues in different social, cultural arenas.

He has been a Key contributor to over 50 Brands in four continents thus far and has initiated Strategic leadership for multiple startups among others. At present, he is utilizing his expertise at StartupX Foundry as the Program Manager where he gets to work with a few startups that are being incubated/accelerated as their primary mentor and facilitator.
